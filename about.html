About

Born and raised in Mumbai, India, I have always been exposed to several possible professions. However, the hospitality sector always fascinated me. Following a few months of training under the Taj Hotels Group, I felt myself striking a balance with service and satisfaction. I went on to learn more in the field when I continued my studies in Switzerland.

With the onset of the pandemic, I started looking for new avenues to challenge myself. I stumbled upon the world of programming and I realized it was a happy accident. The spark I was missing ignited the instant I clicked play on my first JavaScript tutorial video. Since then, I cannot get enough. Front-end web development incorporates creativity, enthusiasm, curiosity and problem solving.

I love applying responsive design principles and watching my web pages shrink into mobile screens. The very feeling of my website working, that I have built from scratch, is mind-boggling. My specialties include learning new skills and programming languages. I believe in challenging myself and leaving my comfort zone. I am still enthusiastically grabbing onto any other programming languages, frameworks and principles that I can integrate into the coding web in my head.
